# The Black Emperor

**Command:** `!namedcampaign the-black-emperor`
**Setting:** Dragonlance / Krynn
**Unlock requirement:** 5 standard campaign clears
**Difficulty:** Higher than the standard gauntlet
**Cooldown:** Shares the channel daily cooldown

## Lore

Years after the War of the Lance, the banners are wrong.

Not unfamiliar — that is the problem. They are the banners of a war that ended, raised again by hands that should not exist. Villages fall under eerily familiar colors. Survivors speak of a Dragon Highlord who does not die. Priests report disturbances tied to Takhisis that have no explanation.

A name resurfaces. And beneath it, a truth: Ariakas has returned — not as a ghost of the past, but as a living warlord sustained by dark will and the corrupted favor of the Dark Queen. He has studied every failure. He has corrected every flaw.

He has rebuilt a general that cannot question, cannot hesitate, and exists only to conquer.

The party must break the cycle before it becomes unstoppable — or decide whether breaking it is truly what they intend to do.

## Starting the Campaign

`!namedcampaign ashes-of-the-black-emperor`

You will be prompted to choose `!solo` or `!joincamp`. Named campaigns require at least 5 standard `!campaign` clears to unlock. If you have not yet completed the required number, you will be turned away.

## The Five Stages

Rest shrines appear before stages 2 through 5.

Brother Yvannis appears at one random stage (2–4) alongside his shrine.

| Stage | Name | Enemy | Special |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Echoes of the Dragonarmies | Dragonarmy Vanguard (x2) | None |
| 2 | The Perfect Weapon | Cloned Verminaard's Enforcers (x2) | Domination Strike — heavy hit on one target |
| 3 | The Black Emperor Returns | Dragonarmy Elite (x2) | Dragon Fire — hits all party members |
| 4 | The Engine of Domination | The Cloned Verminaard | Fanatic's Will — hits all party members |
| 5 | The Emperor Eternal | Ariakas, Black Emperor | Avatar of Domination — hits all party members |

## The Ending Vote

After Ariakas is defeated, the campaign does not end immediately. Surviving participants vote on what to do with the system he built and what Krynn becomes next.

**Vote window:** 3 minutes, participants only.

**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the vote opens to all of chat for a further 3 minutes.

**Final tiebreaker:** If still tied, fate picks randomly.

Type the number of your choice during the vote window.

Some titles are locked to specific vote outcomes.

::: danger Permadeath
⛔ Death during a named campaign is permanent. If your HP reaches 0 at any stage you are eliminated for the rest of that run. Use `!rest` between campaigns to keep your HP healthy going in.
:::

::: warning Consequences
⚠️ Every ending writes a persistent consequence to your character that carries into future campaigns. The bleak ending carries the heaviest toll in the game. Choose with full awareness of what you are choosing.
:::
