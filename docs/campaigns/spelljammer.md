# The Dying Star

**Command:** `!namedcampaign the-dying-star`
**Setting:** Spelljammer / Wildspace
**Unlock requirement:** 3 standard campaign clears
**Difficulty:** Higher than the standard gauntlet
**Cooldown:** Shares the channel daily cooldown

## Lore

Across Wildspace, worlds are going dark.

Not all at once. Slowly — crops failing, sunlight thinning, strange crystalline growths pushing up through soil that was healthy yesterday. By the time a world understands what is happening to it, the process is already nearly complete.

The Xaryxian Empire feeds their dying star through the Starfire Engine, an arcane construct of staggering scale that harvests the life energy of entire planets to keep Xaryxis burning. It has done this for centuries. Your world is next on the list.

A spelljamming vessel arrives — or crashes — carrying news of what is coming. The party has a narrow window to act. Find the Engine. Reach it. And decide, when you are standing in front of it, what you are actually willing to do.

## Starting the Campaign

`!namedcampaign ashes-of-xaryxis`

You will be prompted to choose `!solo` or `!joincamp`. Named campaigns require at least 3 standard `!campaign` clears to unlock. If you have not yet completed the required number, you will be turned away.

## The Five Stages

Rest shrines appear before stages 2 through 5.

Brother Yvannis appears at one random stage (2–4) alongside his shrine.

| Stage | Name | Enemy | Special |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | The Withering | Xaryxian Harvesters (x2) | None |
| 2 | Imperial Patrol | Xaryxian War Skiff Crew (x2) | Coordinated Strike — hits all party members |
| 3 | The Void Between | Void Knight | Starfire Pulse — hits all party members |
| 4 | The Vanguard of Xeleth | Xeleth's Vanguard Commander | Engine-Bonded — heavy hit on one target |
| 5 | The Engine and the Heir | Prince Xeleth, Engine-Fused | Reality Fracture — hits all party members |

## The Ending Vote

After Prince Xeleth is defeated, the campaign does not end immediately. Surviving participants vote on what to do with the Starfire Engine.

**Vote window:** 3 minutes, participants only.

**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the vote opens to all of chat for a further 3 minutes.

**Final tiebreaker:** If still tied, fate picks randomly.

Type the number of your choice during the vote window.

Some titles are locked to specific vote outcomes.

::: danger Permadeath
⛔ Death during a named campaign is permanent. If your HP reaches 0 at any stage you are eliminated for the rest of that run. Use `!rest` between campaigns to keep your HP healthy going in.
:::
