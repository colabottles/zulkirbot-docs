# The Crystal of Rafiel

**Command:** `!campaign mystara`
**Setting:**  Mystara / Hollow World
**Unlock requirement:** 10 standard campaign clears
**Difficulty:** Higher than the standard gauntlet
**Cooldown:** Shares the channel daily cooldown

## Lore

Deep beneath the world of Mystara, in the lightless realms of the Shadow Elves, the sacred Crystal of Rafiel is failing. For centuries it has done more than guide — it has stabilized the Shadow Elves themselves, shielding them from the invisible corruption left behind by the ancient catastrophe known as the Great Rain of Fire.

Now it flickers. Priests report distorted visions. The Fourteen Verses contradict themselves. Some elves are mutating. Some are vanishing.

The rival Immortal Atzanteotl reaches through the dark, poisoning the Crystal's signal, slowly twisting an entire civilization into fanatic servants. The party must enter the Chamber of the Spheres, uncover the truth, and decide the fate of the Shadow Elves.

## Starting the Campaign

`!campaign mystara`

You will be prompted to choose `!solo` or `!joincamp`. Named campaigns require at least one standard !campaign clear to unlock. If you have not yet completed a standard campaign, you will be turned away.

## The Five Stages

Rest shrines appear before stages 2 through 5.

Brother Yvannis appears at one random stage (2–4) alongside his shrine.

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Stage | Name | Enemy | Special |
| 1 | The Descent| Shadow Elf | Border Wardens (x2) | None
| 2 | The Broken Faith | Corrupted Shadow Elf Acolyte | Radiance Pulse — hits all party members
| 3 | The Blackmoor Ruins | Blackmoor Construct| Null Field — suppresses one player's attack
| 4 | The Rogue Priest | High Priest Sylvael | Fanatic's Brand — marks one player as sole target
| 5 | The Chamber of the Spheres | The Echo of Atzanteotl | The Unmaking Verse — hits all party members for 35 damage

## The Ending Vote

After the Echo of Atzanteotl is defeated, the campaign does not end immediately. Surviving participants vote on what to do with the Crystal of Rafiel.

**Vote window:** *3 minutes*, participants only.
**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the vote opens to all of chat for a further 3 minutes.
**Final tiebreaker:** If still tied, fate picks randomly.

Type the number of your choice during the vote window.

Some titles are locked to specific vote outcomes.

::: danger Permadeath
Death during a named campaign is permanent. If your HP reaches 0 at any stage you are eliminated for the rest of that run. Use `!rest` between campaigns to keep your HP healthy going in.
:::
