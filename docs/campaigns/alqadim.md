# The Seal of the Incomparable

**Command:** `!campaign alqadim`
**Setting:** Al-Qadim / Zakhara, the Golden Wastes
**Unlock requirement:** 10 standard campaign clears
**Difficulty:** Higher than the standard gauntlet
**Cooldown:** Shares the channel daily cooldown

## Lore

The Amber Road Compact's caravans are vanishing. The desert is wrong. Sandstorms move
against the wind. Elementals flicker and vanish. Genies have gone silent across Zakhara,
and the ones who still speak will not say why.

The Compact hires the party to investigate — not to find an artifact, not to stop a war,
just to find out what is happening to the trade routes they depend on. What the party
discovers in the deep desert points to something buried far beneath the Golden Wastes: the
vault of Sulayman al-Rasikh, the first and greatest sha'ir, who bound all genie-kind to
his will with a single artifact and called it order.

The genies united, took the Seal, and hid it. That was centuries ago. Now two factions
are moving toward it — the Emerald Concordat, scholars who believe they can control it,
and the Ashen Throne, efreeti nobles who simply want it. Neither knows what the Seal was
actually for. Neither knows what it has been holding back.

## Starting the Campaign

```plaintext
!campaign alqadim
```

You will be prompted to choose `!solo` or `!joincamp`. Named campaigns require 10
standard `!campaign` clears to unlock. If you have not yet reached that threshold, you
will be turned away.

## The Five Stages

Rest shrines appear before stages 2 through 5. Brother Yvannis appears at one random
stage (2–4) alongside his shrine.

| Stage | Name | Enemy | Special |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | The Shifting Sands | Amber Road Raiders (x2) | None |
| 2 | The Rogue Sha'ir | Sulayman's Bound Sha'ir | Elemental Lash — hits all party members |
| 3 | The Genie Trials | Trial Warden of the Dao | Stone Mandate — reduces one player's damage |
| 4 | The Vault of Sulayman | Efreeti Vault Lord | Sultan's Wrath — marks one player as sole target |
| 5 | The Seal Awakens | The First Sultan — Primordial Convergence | The Unmaking Wind — hits all party members for 40 damage |

## The Ending Vote

After the Primordial Convergence is defeated the campaign does not end immediately.
Surviving participants vote on what to do with the Seal of the Incomparable.

Both the Emerald Concordat and the Ashen Throne are present. Neither has a vote.

**Vote window:** 3 minutes, participants only.
**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the vote opens to all of chat for a further 3 minutes.
**Final tiebreaker:** If still tied, fate picks randomly.

Type the number of your choice during the vote window.

| # | Choice |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Use the Seal |
| 2 | Destroy the Seal |
| 3 | Return it to the Elemental Planes |

Each choice carries consequences. Some are known immediately. Some are not.

## Rewards

| Reward | Condition |
| --- | --- |
| XP (75–400, scaling per stage) | All participants, for stages survived |
| Gold (30–160, scaling per stage) | All participants, for stages survived |
| Full clear bonus (+350 XP, +150 gold) | Survivors of all 5 stages only |
| Unique title | All survivors — drawn from the campaign title pool |
| The Seal of the Incomparable (artifact) | One random survivor of the boss fight |

::: danger Permadeath
Death during a named campaign is permanent. If your HP reaches 0 at any stage you are
eliminated for the rest of that run. Use `!rest` between campaigns to keep your HP
healthy going in.
:::
