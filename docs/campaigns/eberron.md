# Ashes Beneath the Flame

**Command:** `!namedcampaign ashes-beneath-the-flame`
**Setting:** Eberron / Thrane
**Unlock requirement:** 3 standard campaign clears
**Difficulty:** Higher than the standard gauntlet
**Cooldown:** Shares the channel daily cooldown

## Lore

In the theocracy of Thrane, faith in the Silver Flame is absolute.

The party aids in what seems like holy work — purging a suspected cult, intercepting a heretic, securing a sacred relic. The missions are clear. The cause is righteous. The guidance is divine.

Afterward, the evidence does not add up. The people who died were not who they were supposed to be. Each party member begins experiencing shared visions of flame and shadow.

They are marked.

The investigation leads deeper into the Church of the Silver Flame — into conflicting orders, altered records, trusted allies who are not what they appear. The pattern is not outside the Church. It has never been outside the Church. And at the center of it is something that has been doing this, patiently and without malice, for two hundred years.

The party must decide what to do with what they find. And whether they can still trust their own judgment when they find it.

## Starting the Campaign

`!namedcampaign ashes-beneath-the-flame`

You will be prompted to choose `!solo` or `!joincamp`. Named campaigns require at least 3 standard `!campaign` clears to unlock. If you have not yet completed the required number, you will be turned away.

## The Five Stages

Rest shrines appear before stages 2 through 5.

Brother Yvannis appears at one random stage (2–4) alongside his shrine.

| Stage | Name | Enemy | Special |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | The Mark That Burns Cold | Whispering Flame Cultists (x2) | None |
| 2 | Faith in Fracture | Compromised Church Inquisitors (x2) | False Judgment — heavy hit on one target |
| 3 | The Wyrmbreaker | Durastoran's Thralls (x2) | Paranoia Pulse — hits all party members |
| 4 | The False Light | Corrupted Flame Wardens | Brazier Surge — hits all party members |
| 5 | The Shadow Unbound | Durastoran the Wyrmbreaker | The Whispering — hits all party members |

## The Ending Vote

After Durastoran is defeated, the campaign does not end immediately. Surviving participants vote on what to do with the Brazier and what remains of the Silver Flame.

**Vote window:** 3 minutes, participants only.

**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the vote opens to all of chat for a further 3 minutes.

**Final tiebreaker:** If still tied, fate picks randomly.

Type the number of your choice during the vote window.

Some titles are locked to specific vote outcomes.

::: danger Permadeath
Death during a named campaign is permanent. If your HP reaches 0 at any stage you are eliminated for the rest of that run. Use `!rest` between campaigns to keep your HP healthy going in.
:::
