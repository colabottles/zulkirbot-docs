# The Shattered Memory of Darkon

**Command:** `!namedcampaign shattered-memory-of-darkon`
**Setting:** Ravenloft / Darkon
**Unlock requirement:** 5 standard campaign clears
**Difficulty:** Higher than the standard gauntlet
**Cooldown:** Shares the channel daily cooldown

## Lore

The party awakens in a quiet town. There are people who know their names. There are routines that feel familiar. There is no memory of arriving.

Darkon is a Domain of Dread — a realm sealed by the Mists, ruled by the lich-king Azalin Rex. In Darkon, memory is not reliable. The longer you stay, the more of yourself you lose. The land rewrites its inhabitants slowly, replacing what they were with what the domain needs them to be.

Azalin Rex is not what he was either. After decades of catastrophic magical experiments aimed at breaking his own curse, his essence has fractured. Part of him remains the tyrant lich. Part of him wanders as Firan Zal'honan — a brilliant, haunted scholar who does not fully understand what he is. His grip on the domain is weakening, and as it weakens, Darkon comes apart.

The party must find a way out before they forget who they are entirely. To do that, they will have to confront a lich-king who is no longer whole, in a domain where reality itself is no longer trustworthy.

## Starting the Campaign

`!namedcampaign shattered-memory-of-darkon`

You will be prompted to choose `!solo` or `!joincamp`. Named campaigns require at least 5 standard `!campaign` clears to unlock. If you have not yet completed the required number, you will be turned away.

## The Five Stages

Rest shrines appear before stages 2 through 5.

Brother Yvannis appears at one random stage (2–4) alongside his shrine.

| Stage | Name | Enemy | Special |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | The Stolen Self | Kargat Agents (x2) | None |
| 2 | Echoes of the Lich-King | Kargat Inquisitors (x2) | Memory Drain — heavy hit on one target |
| 3 | The Broken King | Fractured Remnants of Firan | Identity Shatter — hits all party members |
| 4 | The Curse Unraveled | Azalin's Undead Legion | Necromantic Surge — hits all party members |
| 5 | The King of Broken Truths | Azalin Ascendant | Curse of the Broken King — hits all party members |

## The Ending Vote

After Azalin Ascendant is defeated, the campaign does not end immediately. Surviving participants vote on what to do with Azalin and the curse.

**Vote window:** 3 minutes, participants only.

**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the vote opens to all of chat for a further 3 minutes.

**Final tiebreaker:** If still tied, fate picks randomly.

Type the number of your choice during the vote window.

Some titles are locked to specific vote outcomes.

::: danger Permadeath
⛔ Death during a named campaign is permanent. If your HP reaches 0 at any stage you are eliminated for the rest of that run. Use `!rest` between campaigns to keep your HP healthy going in.
:::
