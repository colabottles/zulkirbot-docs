# Getting Started

## !join

Create your character. Pick a class or get one assigned randomly.
`!join`
`!join wizard`
`!join dark_apostate`

## !status

View your character stats. You can check another player's stats too.
`!status`
`!status @username`

Your status shows your name, prestige rank, class, level, HP, XP, and gold.

## !help

List all available commands. `!help`
