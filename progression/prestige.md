# Prestige

Reached Level 40? You can transcend mortal limits and prestige.

## !prestige

**Requirements:** Level 40 and 1,000g

**What you lose:**

- Your level resets to 1
- Your XP resets to 0
- 1,000g prestige cost

**What you keep:**

- All gold (minus the cost)
- All inventory items
- Your bank vault

**What you gain:**

- +10 permanent max HP
- A prestige rank badge shown in `!status`

## Prestige Ranks

| Rank | Badge |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | [Epic I] |
| 2 | [Epic II] |
| 3 | [Epic III] |
| 4 | [Epic IV] |
| 5 | [Epic V] |

> [!TIP]
> The maximum prestige rank is **Epic V**. Reaching it makes you a legend.
