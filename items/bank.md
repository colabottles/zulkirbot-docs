# The Bank

Store up to **50 items** in your personal vault. Your bank is wiped on permadeath.

| Command | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `!bank deposit [item]` | Deposit an unequipped item into your vault. |
| `!bank depositall` | Deposit all unequipped items at once. |
| `!bank withdraw [item]` | Retrieve an item from your vault. |
| `!bank list` | View everything stored in your vault. |
