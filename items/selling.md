# Selling Items

## !sell [item]

Sell an unequipped item for gold.

| Rarity | Base Price |
| --- | --- |
| Common | 10g |
| Uncommon | 30g |
| Rare | 60g |
| Legendary | 150g |

> [!TIP]
> Rare and Legendary items roll a d100 on sale. Roll **75 or higher** and you get **double** the price!

## !sell all

Sell all unequipped, non-cursed items at once.
